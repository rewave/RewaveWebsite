What is Rewave ?

Glad you asked :)

It's an app that connects with your computer and lets you control slides as you move your phone, giving you the ability to move freely (you don't need to stand infront of your computer to click a button) and present with style.

It's like a magic wand.

How does it works ?

It connects with your computer running a small software called 'Rewave Server'. When you move your hand, out advanced motion detection algorithm recognises your motion as 'left wave' or 'right wave' and sends a signal to the server. The server then simulates a key press.

Ex. If left wave is detected, the server will know that you want to proceed to next slide and hence press the left key.

The key mapping approach allows us to extend this system to any presentation viewer : Google Docs, MS PPT, Slideshare, Rewave works with all.
