About

Rewave is an app that connects with your computer and lets you control slides as you move your phone, giving you the ability to move freely (you don't need to stand infront of your computer to click a button) and present with style.

It's wireless, like a magic wand. You can think of it as Nintendo Wii but without extra hardware.

Rewave works with Google Docs, Microsoft PPT, Slideshare, Prezi (and all others).
